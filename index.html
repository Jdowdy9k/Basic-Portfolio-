About Me

I'm a craftsman by trade and builder in general. I've always been creating or destroying things, often times destroying in order to create. And in case of the cartoon robot Models I build at least, this cycle of events tends to be never ending. I've always been a proponate of the philosophy "work hard, play harder" which could explain why I take my video games so seriously, also why I sometimes pass out with a tiny paint brush and robot in my hands. To sum me up in a word i would use "Adventurer" not because I travel a lot or wonder, but because my heart and mind are restless and i simply must see more.
